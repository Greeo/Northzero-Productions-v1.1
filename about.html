Our Philosophy

At Northzero Productions, we believe that every structure has a heartbeat and every product has a tactile soul. We don't just capture images; we archive the structural integrity and emotional resonance of a space.

The Preservationist's Perspective

Founded by Omar A. Greene, a member of the Village of Ossining Historic Preservation Commission, Northzero is built on a foundation of architectural respect. Our background in preservation means we approach Real Estate and Design films with a unique understanding of "The Bones"—the historical and structural elements that make a property truly valuable.
