Our Philosophy

At Northzero Productions, we believe that every structure has a heartbeat. Our work is rooted in over a decade of integrated design, cinematic production, and a deep respect for the built environment — translating architectural intent into visual permanence.

We don't separate design from film. The same eye that shapes a brand identity frames a landmark estate. The same understanding of light that drives a motion graphics sequence informs how we document a historic property for institutional review.

Omar A. Greene

Founder of Northzero Productions and alternate member of the Village of Ossining Historic Preservation Commission. A Parsons the New School for Design alumnus, Omar brings over ten years of integrated design experience to every production — spanning front-end development, brand identity, motion graphics, lighting design, sound, and cinematography.

His background is rare: a filmmaker who is equally at home writing production code, directing a luxury estate shoot, or presenting visual documentation before a municipal review board. That breadth is the foundation of Northzero's distinctive approach — technical rigour in service of cinematic permanence.

Based in the New York City Metropolitan Area, serving Westchester County, the Hudson Valley, and the Tri-State region.
